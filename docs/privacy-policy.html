Beta Log — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

Data Storage

Beta Log stores all data locally on your device. Your workout logs, timer presets, and settings never leave your phone.

Data Collection

We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. No account or sign-up is required to use the app.

Analytics & Tracking

Beta Log does not include any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks.

Third-Party Links

The app contains a single optional external link to Buy Me a Coffee, a third-party service. If you choose to visit that link, their own privacy policy applies.
